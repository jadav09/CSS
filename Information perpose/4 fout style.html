vw/vh : viewport-width/height

vh stands for viewport height. This unit is based on the height of the viewport. A value of 1 vh is equal to 1% of the viewport height. A value of 100vh is equal to 100% of the viewport height.

1vw stands for viewport width. This unit is based on the width of the viewport. A value of 1vw is equal to 1% of the viewport width.
